Our Mission

At Ricked & Rolled, our mission is simple: to never give you up, never let you down, and definitely never warn you before it’s too late. We are the internet’s premiere archive, shrine, and trapdoor dedicated to the fine art of the rickroll. From vintage bait-and-clicks to cutting-edge meme warfare, we celebrate every glorious moment when someone thought they were getting free V-Bucks or breaking news—only to be met with a dancing Rick in glorious 480p.

This site exists to document the history, evolution, and pure chaotic joy of one of the internet’s oldest and greatest pranks. Whether you're here to relive the classics, find inspiration for your next unsuspecting victim, or just accidentally got tricked into learning something—consider yourself part of the rickrolling legacy now. We’re not just a website. We’re a movement. A promise. A pixelated legend. Because when the world changes, trends fade, and memes come and go… Rick Astley remains.
